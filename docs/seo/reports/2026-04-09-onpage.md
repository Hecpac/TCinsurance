## P0 — Critical Issues

### Blog /article `about` field uses raw category string instead of product name

- **Page(s):** All blog posts without a specific `about` override (e.g., `/blog/como-elegir-plan-de-salud-en-texas`, `/blog/seguro-de-vida-liquidez-familiar`, `/blog/agente-seguros-salud-lewisville-tx`, and others)
- **Impact:** The `Article` schema's `about` field emits `{ "@type": "InsuranceProduct", "name": "SALUD" }` — a raw category constant, not a meaningful insurance product name. Google uses this for entity recognition and may demote the article's relevance for product-level searches.
- **Current state:** In `src/app/blog/[slug]/page.tsx` (line 253–256), the fallback for `about.name` is `post.category`, which is the uppercase label like "SALUD", "VIDA", "NOTICIAS". Only the `seguro-gastos-finales` slug has a specific override.
- **Suggested fix:**

  Add a `aboutProduct` field to `BlogPost` data or expand the override logic in `page.tsx`:

  ```tsx
  // In src/app/blog/[slug]/page.tsx — articleJsonLd about field
  about: {
    "@type": "InsuranceProduct",
    name:
      post.aboutProduct ??   // new optional field in blogPosts.ts
      ({
        SALUD: "Seguro de salud en Texas",
        VIDA: "Seguro de vida en Texas",
        LEGADO: "Seguro de gastos finales en Texas",
        MEDICARE: "Medicare en Texas",
        NOTICIAS: "Seguros en Texas",
      }[post.category] ?? post.category),
  },
  ```
