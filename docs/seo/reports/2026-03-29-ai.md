## P0 — Critical Issues

### 1. No AggregateRating schema despite 6 rated testimonials

**Impact:** AI assistants and Google AI Overviews heavily weight review signals when recommending service businesses. Without `AggregateRating` schema, TC Insurance cannot appear in "best insurance agent in Lewisville TX" or "top Spanish insurance agent Dallas" queries. The data already exists in `src/data/testimonials.ts` — it is simply not being emitted as schema.

**Fix:** Compute the aggregate in `LocalBusinessJsonLd.tsx` from the `testimonials` array and emit the `aggregateRating` block on the `InsuranceAgency` entity. Also create individual `Review` schema for each testimonial.

### 2. Global FAQ (`src/data/faq.ts`) does not cover the most common AI queries for the target audience

**Impact:** The homepage FAQ schema (9 questions) misses the 5–6 highest-volume questions users ask AI assistants about insurance for Spanish-speaking families in Texas. When ChatGPT or Perplexity answers "¿puedo obtener seguro sin papeles?" or "¿cuánto cuesta un seguro de salud en Texas?", TC Insurance is not the source because those questions are not answered on the page the FAQ schema is attached to (the homepage).

**Fix:** Expand `src/data/faq.ts` to 18–24 questions, adding the 10 gap questions identified in the FAQ analysis above. Prioritize: cost ranges, eligibility without documents, enrollment dates, and the "is the agent free?" question.

### 3. No external authority signals (no BBB, no Google Business Profile, no third-party citations)

**Impact:** LLMs build entity knowledge from structured data they encounter across the web. If TC Insurance only appears on its own website, it has a weak entity graph. ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini are significantly more likely to cite businesses that have a Google Business Profile (for local knowledge), a BBB listing, and are mentioned by local media or directories.

**Fix:**
- Create and verify a Google Business Profile for TC Insurance Agency Services, LLC in Lewisville TX. Add it to `sameAs` in schema.
- Submit a BBB listing.
- Submit to local Hispanic directories (negocios.yellowpages.com, directoriolatino.com, yellowpages.com).

**Impact:** LLMs and Google AI Overviews strongly prefer content with visible freshness signals. The service pages (`/servicios/seguro-salud-texas`, etc.) have no published date. When competing with content from BCBS, UnitedHealth, or Healthcare.gov, lack of a date is a significant disadvantage.
