## P0 — Critical Issues (All Domains)

### 1. Noindex pages in sitemap.xml
**Domain:** Technical Audit
`/privacidad` and `/terminos` are in `sitemap.ts` but served with `robots: noindex`. Contradicts Google's crawl signals.
**Fix:** Remove both from `staticRoutes` in `src/app/sitemap.ts` (5 min).

### 2. Global WebPage schema hardcoded to homepage on all pages
**Domain:** Technical Audit
`LocalBusinessJsonLd.tsx` injects a `WebPage` node with `@id: /#home` and `url: tcinsurancetx.com` on all 24 non-homepage pages. Corrupts the page entity graph.
**Fix:** Remove the `WebPage` node from `LocalBusinessJsonLd.tsx` (10 min).

### 3. Footer headings rendered as H3 — pollutes heading hierarchy
**Domain:** On-Page
Footer labels (Oficina, Navegacion, Legal, Social) are `<h3>` elements, inflating H3 count to 30 on homepage. Services carousel also duplicates 7 H3s.
**Fix:** Change footer H3s to `<p>` tags. Add `aria-hidden="true"` to the duplicate carousel copy.

### 4. /blog H1 is non-descriptive brand copy
**Domain:** On-Page
H1 = "Blog estrategico." — no keyword, no topic, no location signal.
**Fix:** Change to `<h1>Guias de Seguros de Salud y Vida en Texas</h1>`.

### 5. Homepage lacks extractable business definition
**Domain:** AI/GEO
The h1 is a marketing slogan. AIs cannot answer "what does TC Insurance do?" from the homepage.
**Fix:** Add entity definition sentence: "TC Insurance es una agencia de seguros licenciada en Texas..."

### 6. No AggregateRating schema despite 6 testimonials
**Domain:** AI/GEO
6 testimonials exist (avg 4.83/5) but no structured rating data. Blocks rich result eligibility.
**Fix:** Add `AggregateRating` to `LocalBusinessJsonLd.tsx` from `testimonials.ts` data.

### 7. License number absent from root structured data
**Domain:** AI/GEO
TDI #2629251 and NPN #19630324 appear only as prose on `/sobre-mi`. AIs reading schema cannot confirm licensure.
**Fix:** Add `hasCredential` and `identifier` to `InsuranceAgency` entity in `LocalBusinessJsonLd.tsx`.
