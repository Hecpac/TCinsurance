# Partnership Ads (Run Through Creator)

Ads published through a creator/partner's account using Meta's Partnership Ads feature.

## Why Partnership Ads

- Access the creator's audience and credibility
- Different voice and perspective than brand ads
- Often get better CPMs due to organic-feeling content
- Algorithmic boost from diverse ad sources

## Potential Partners for TC Insurance

| Partner Type | Example | Content Angle |
|-------------|---------|---------------|
| Latino community influencer (DFW) | Local lifestyle creator | "Mi agente me ayudo a encontrar..." |
| Small business owner | Client who uses TC Insurance | Testimonial from their perspective |
| Financial literacy creator | Money/budgeting influencer | "How I got health insurance for $0" |
| Real estate agent | Referral partner | "What I tell my clients about insurance" |

## Setup

1. Creator creates the content
2. TC Insurance requests Partnership Ad access via Meta Business Suite
3. Creator approves the partnership
4. TC Insurance runs the ad with their budget, from creator's handle
5. Both parties see performance metrics

## Budget

- Allocate 15-20% of ad budget to partnership ads
- Compensate creators with flat fee per piece of content
