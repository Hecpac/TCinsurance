# Motion Graphics

Animated ads with text, icons, and transitions. No talking head, no live footage.

## When to Use

- Countdown timers (enrollment deadlines)
- Data visualization (price comparisons, savings charts)
- Step-by-step processes (how to enroll)
- Attention-grabbing feed stoppers

## TC Insurance Motion Graphics Ideas

1. Animated price comparison: "Sin subsidio: $600/mes → Con subsidio: $0/mes"
2. Enrollment countdown clock ticking down
3. Map of DFW with service areas lighting up
4. Step 1-2-3 enrollment process animation
5. Dollar bills flying away vs. savings growing (with/without agent)

## Production Notes

- Keep under 15 seconds for Reels placement
- Bold text, high contrast, dark bg with blue accents
- Add urgency through motion (ticking, counting, sliding)
- Always include subtitles/text — no audio dependency
- Export in 1:1, 4:5, and 9:16

## Tools

- Canva Pro (templates, quick turnaround)
- After Effects (custom, higher quality)
- CapCut (fast mobile editing)
