# Yapper Videos (Talking Head, No Edit)

Tatiana talks directly to camera. No fancy editing. Authenticity wins.

## Why Yapper Videos Work

- Build personal connection with the audience
- Establish Tatiana as the expert (E-E-A-T signal for brand)
- Feel native to social feeds (not "ad-like")
- Cheapest video format to produce

## Format

- Length: 30-60 seconds (under 15s for Reels)
- Orientation: 9:16 vertical (phone)
- Setup: ring light, phone camera, clean background
- No script reading — bullet points only
- Subtitles always (80%+ watch without sound)

## Topic Ideas for TC Insurance

1. "3 errores al elegir seguro de salud"
2. "Que pasa si pierdes Medicaid en Texas"
3. "Subsidios ACA 2026: lo que cambio"
4. "Freelancer? Asi consigues seguro"
5. "Medicare vs. Marketplace: cual es para ti"
6. "Tu seguro de vida deberia ser 10x tu salario"

## Hook Formula

- First 3 seconds must hook: question, bold statement, or "Stop scrolling if..."
- Examples: "Si vives en Texas y no tienes seguro..." / "Esto nadie te dice sobre Medicare..."
