# Frequency Above 2.5 over 7 Days

If the average person sees your ad more than 2.5 times in a week, it's too much.

## Benchmarks

| Frequency (7-day) | Status | Action |
|--------------------|--------|--------|
| 1.0 - 1.5 | Ideal | Stay the course |
| 1.5 - 2.0 | Normal | Monitor trends |
| 2.0 - 2.5 | Caution | Prepare new creatives |
| 2.5 - 3.0 | Warning | Refresh creatives now |
| 3.0+ | Critical | Pause and rebuild |

## Effects of High Frequency

- Ad blindness (people stop noticing your ads)
- Negative sentiment (annoyed users hide/report ads)
- Rising CPMs (Meta penalizes low-engagement ads)
- Declining CTR and conversion rate
- Brand damage (perceived as spammy)

## Immediate Actions

1. Pause the 2-3 oldest/worst-performing ads
2. Add 3-5 new creative concepts
3. Check audience size — may be too small for the budget
4. Reduce daily budget temporarily if audience is capped
5. Expand targeting if audience is exhausted

## Prevention

- Minimum audience size: 100k+ for prospecting
- Rotate creative every 3-4 weeks
- Keep 4-6 active ads per ad set (diversity reduces per-ad frequency)
