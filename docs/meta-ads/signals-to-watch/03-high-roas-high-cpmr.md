# High ROAS + High CPMr (Warning)

This combination looks good on paper but signals a problem.

## What It Means

- High ROAS: your ads are converting well
- High CPMr: you're reaching the same people repeatedly
- Translation: you're squeezing conversions from a shrinking audience
- This is NOT sustainable — performance will cliff suddenly

## The Trap

```
Week 1: ROAS 4x, CPMr $10 → Healthy
Week 2: ROAS 5x, CPMr $15 → Looks better! (but isn't)
Week 3: ROAS 6x, CPMr $22 → Danger zone
Week 4: ROAS 2x, CPMr $30 → Cliff (audience exhausted)
```

## Why It Happens

- You're only reaching warm/engaged users repeatedly
- Each conversion comes from a smaller pool
- When that pool is exhausted, ROAS crashes overnight

## Action

- Don't celebrate high ROAS without checking CPMr
- If both are rising together, immediately:
  1. Refresh 50%+ of creatives
  2. Expand audience (broader targeting, wider geo)
  3. Increase budget by 15% to access new segments
  4. Add new creative formats for fresh algorithmic signals
