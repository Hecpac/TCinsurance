# CPMr Climbing Week on Week

Rising CPMr means you're paying more to reach the same people.

## Trigger

CPMr increases 15%+ for 2 consecutive weeks.

## Diagnosis

| Possible Cause | Check | Fix |
|----------------|-------|-----|
| Creative fatigue | Same ads running 3+ weeks | Add 3-5 new concepts |
| Audience saturation | Net new reach < 50% | Expand geo or refresh exclusions |
| Seasonal competition | Q4, OEP, tax season | Increase budget to compete |
| Frequency too high | Frequency > 2.5 (7 days) | Pause oldest ads, add new |
| Audience too narrow | Custom audience < 50k | Switch to broader targeting |

## Action Sequence

1. Check frequency first — if above 2.5, creative fatigue is likely
2. Check net new reach — if below 50%, audience is saturated
3. Refresh 50%+ of active creatives
4. If still climbing after refresh, expand geographic targeting
5. Last resort: pause campaign for 5-7 days, relaunch with new creative batch
