# Net New Reach % Declining

You're showing ads to the same people instead of finding new ones.

## Trigger

Net new reach drops below 60% for 2 consecutive weeks.

## Why It Happens

- Budget is too high for the audience size
- Creative is stale (algorithm can't find new responsive segments)
- Exclusion lists are outdated (showing to existing clients)
- Geographic targeting is too narrow

## Action Plan

1. Update exclusion lists (CRM upload, recent converters)
2. Add 3-5 new creative concepts with different hooks
3. Check if budget increased recently — may have outpaced audience
4. Consider expanding DFW radius by 10 miles
5. Test a new creative format (if all static, try video or motion)

## Prevention

- Refresh creative batch every 3-4 weeks
- Update exclusion lists monthly
- Monitor net new reach weekly (every Monday)
- Don't scale budget faster than 20% per week
