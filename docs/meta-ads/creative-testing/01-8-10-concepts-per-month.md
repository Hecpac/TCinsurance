# 8-10 Concepts per Month

A "concept" is a unique creative angle or message — not just a different image.

## What Counts as a Concept

- Different hook/message (e.g., "Save money" vs. "Protect your family")
- Different format (static vs. video vs. carousel)
- Different audience angle (freelancers vs. families vs. seniors)
- Different emotional trigger (fear of loss vs. peace of mind vs. urgency)

## TC Insurance Concept Examples

1. "Sin seguro en Texas? Estas son tus opciones" (fear/urgency)
2. Tatiana explaining ACA subsidies (authority/education)
3. Price comparison table — Bronze vs. Silver vs. Gold (logic/value)
4. Client testimonial: "Me ahorro $300/mes" (social proof)
5. Deadline countdown: Open Enrollment ends Jan 15 (scarcity)
6. "Freelancer en DFW? Tu seguro no tiene que ser caro" (niche targeting)
7. Family photo + "Desde $0/mes con subsidio" (aspiration)
8. Video: 3 mistakes when choosing health insurance (education)

## Rules

- Each concept should feel distinctly different from the others
- Don't just change the background color and call it a new concept
- Track which concepts win — double down on winning angles
