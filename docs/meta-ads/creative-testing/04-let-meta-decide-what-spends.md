# Let Meta Decide What Spends

Do not manually allocate budget to specific ads. Let the algorithm optimize.

## Rules

- Never use "equal distribution" in CBO campaigns
- Don't pause ads after 24 hours because they "aren't spending"
- Give each ad at least 1,000 impressions before judging
- If an ad gets 0 spend after 3 days, it's a signal — not a bug

## What to Monitor

- Which ads Meta is choosing to spend on (these are your winners)
- Creative win rate: 10-20% of ads should capture most spend (healthy)
- If ALL ads spend equally, your concepts aren't different enough

## When to Override

- Only kill an ad manually if:
  - CPL is 3x+ your target after 1,000+ impressions
  - CTR is below 0.5% after 2,000+ impressions
  - The ad is getting spend but zero conversions after 5+ days
