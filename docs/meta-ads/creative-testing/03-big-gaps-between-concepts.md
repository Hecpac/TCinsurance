# Big Gaps Between Concepts

Concepts should be radically different from each other. Small tweaks are not new concepts.

## Bad Example (Too Similar)

- Ad A: "Seguro de salud desde $0/mes" on blue background
- Ad B: "Seguro de salud desde $0/mes" on dark background
- Ad C: "Seguro de salud desde $0/mes" with different font

These are variations of ONE concept, not three concepts.

## Good Example (Big Gaps)

- Concept 1: Price comparison table (logical, data-driven)
- Concept 2: Tatiana talking to camera about enrollment (personal, authority)
- Concept 3: Animated countdown to deadline (urgency, motion)
- Concept 4: Client testimonial screenshot (social proof)

## Why Big Gaps Matter

- Meta's algorithm needs distinctly different signals to learn what works
- Similar ads compete against each other and waste budget
- Big gaps reveal which MESSAGE resonates, not which design looks prettier
