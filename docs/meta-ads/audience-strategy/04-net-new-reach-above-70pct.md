# Net New Reach Above 70%

At least 70% of the people your ads reach each week should be seeing them for the first time.

## Why 70%

- Below 70% means you're showing ads to the same people too often
- Frequency fatigue kills performance (ad blindness)
- You want to expand the funnel, not re-saturate it

## How to Check

1. Go to Ads Manager > Columns > Customize
2. Add: Reach, Frequency, Estimated Ad Recall Lift
3. Compare weekly reach to total cumulative reach
4. Net new reach % = (new unique people reached this week) / (total reached this week)

## If Net New Reach Drops Below 70%

1. Refresh creatives (new concepts, not just new colors)
2. Check frequency — if above 2.5 over 7 days, audience is saturated
3. Consider expanding geographic targeting
4. Add new creative formats (if all static, try video)
5. Increase budget gradually (10-20%) to access new audience segments
