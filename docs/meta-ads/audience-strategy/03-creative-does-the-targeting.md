# Creative Does the Targeting

The ad itself filters the audience. Speak directly to your ideal client in the creative.

## How It Works

- A headline saying "Freelancer en Dallas?" only resonates with freelancers in Dallas
- An image of a Latino family naturally attracts Latino families
- Spanish copy self-selects Spanish speakers
- Mentioning "Medicare" filters for 65+ automatically

## TC Insurance Creative Targeting Examples

| Creative Element | Who It Targets |
|-----------------|----------------|
| "Si eres dueno de negocio en DFW..." | Small business owners |
| Tatiana speaking Spanish | Spanish-speaking community |
| "Perdiste tu Medicaid?" | Recent Medicaid loss |
| "Tu primer seguro de vida" | Young families, first-time buyers |
| "Medicare en Texas: lo basico" | 65+ approaching Medicare |
| "$15/mes por indemnizacion" | Price-sensitive, gig workers |

## Why This Is Better Than Interest Targeting

- Interest targeting says "I liked a health insurance page once"
- Creative targeting says "This ad speaks directly to my situation"
- The algorithm learns who engages and finds more people like them
