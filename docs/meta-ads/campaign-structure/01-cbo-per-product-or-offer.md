# CBO per Product or Offer

Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO) — one campaign per product line or offer.

## TC Insurance Products

| Campaign | Offer | Priority |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Seguro de Salud (ACA) | Asesoria gratuita | High (seasonal: Nov-Jan) |
| Seguro de Vida | Cotizacion sin costo | High (evergreen) |
| Gastos Finales | Consulta gratis | Medium |
| Medicare | Enrollment guidance | High (seasonal: Oct-Dec) |
| Dental + Vision | Bundle comparison | Medium |
| Indemnizacion | Desde $15/mes | Low-Medium |

## Rules

- One CBO campaign per product line
- Set daily budget at campaign level, not ad set level
- Let Meta allocate budget across ad sets within the campaign
- Minimum budget: enough for 50 conversions per week per campaign (Meta recommendation)
- Do NOT mix cold and warm audiences in the same campaign
