# Creative Win Rate (10-20% Healthy)

What percentage of your ads capture the majority of spend.

## Formula

```
Win Rate = (Ads getting 80%+ of spend) / (Total active ads) x 100
```

## What It Means

| Win Rate | Diagnosis |
|----------|-----------|
| 10-20% | Healthy — clear winners, most concepts lose (expected) |
| 5-10% | Very selective — algorithm found a strong winner |
| 20-40% | Concepts too similar — not enough differentiation |
| 40%+ | All ads performing equally — no standout, need bolder concepts |

## TC Insurance Tracking

- Check weekly: which ads are getting spend?
- The winning ads reveal which MESSAGE resonates
- Double down on winning angles with new variations
- Kill losing ads after 1,000 impressions with no results

## What a Healthy Account Looks Like

```
10 active ads:
  2 ads getting 80% of spend (WINNERS)
  3 ads getting 15% of spend (decent)
  5 ads getting 5% of spend (losers — pause these)
```
