# CPMr (CPM x Frequency)

CPMr = Cost Per Mille (CPM) multiplied by Frequency. The real cost of reaching someone.

## Formula

```
CPMr = CPM x Frequency
```

## Why CPMr Matters

- CPM alone doesn't tell you if you're reaching NEW people
- High CPM + low frequency = expensive but healthy (new reach)
- Low CPM + high frequency = cheap but wasteful (same people)
- CPMr captures both dimensions in one number

## Example

| Scenario | CPM | Frequency | CPMr | Verdict |
|----------|-----|-----------|------|---------|
| A | $8 | 1.2 | $9.60 | Healthy |
| B | $5 | 3.0 | $15.00 | Saturated |
| C | $12 | 1.1 | $13.20 | Expensive but healthy |

## Benchmarks for Insurance (DFW)

- Healthy CPMr: $8-$15
- Warning: $15-$25
- Saturated: $25+

## Track Weekly

- Export CPMr every Monday
- Compare week over week
- Rising CPMr = signal to refresh creative or expand audience
