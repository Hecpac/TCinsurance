# Net New Reach % Week on Week

Percentage of people reached this week who haven't seen your ads before.

## How to Calculate

```
Net New Reach % = (Reach this week - Overlap with prior weeks) / Reach this week x 100
```

## Healthy Range

- > 70%: Healthy, scaling well
- 50-70%: Caution, creative fatigue may be starting
- < 50%: Action required, audience is saturated

## How to Monitor in Ads Manager

1. Set date range to "This week" vs. "Last week"
2. Compare unique reach numbers
3. Check frequency trend (rising frequency = declining new reach)

## Actions When Declining

| Net New Reach | Action |
|---------------|--------|
| 70%+ | Stay the course |
| 50-70% | Add 2-3 new concepts |
| 30-50% | Refresh 50%+ of creatives, check exclusions |
| < 30% | Pause, rebuild creative batch, consider new campaign |
